# Oracle One-Pager: StreetCart KC Financial Model ## 1. Executive Summary: The $500,000 Contract StreetCart KC is a 60-day food access pilot, functioning as a municipal contract with fixed deliverables and performance-based KPIs. This is not a grant; it is a direct investment in civic infrastructure with a target of 5,000–7,500 total deliveries for a fully-loaded cost of $20–$35 per box. --- ## 2. Allocation of Funds ($500K) | Category | Amount | Core Expenditures | | :--- | :--- | :--- | | **Pilot Operations** | **$150,000** | 3 FTE equivalents (Director, Ops, Bilingual Liaison), supplemental protein/produce, cold-chain totes/coolers, and van rentals. | | **Tech Build** | **$120,000** | SaaS dispatch & gamification license, kiosk API integration for Smart City Media displays, and last-mile routing software. | | **Marketing & Brand** | **$100,000** | Campaign creative, sports team co-branding (Chiefs, Royals, Sporting, Current), and "Voto y Verdura" bilingual outreach in KCK. | | **Vehicle Wrap** | **$80,000** | Full vehicle wrap for the winning team for one full season, plus maintenance and a renewal runway for Season 2. | | **Evaluation** | **$50,000** | Independent third-party audit, pre/post USDA 6-item food security module, and ZIP-level heat-map analysis. | --- ## 3. Unit Economics & Leverage * **Cost Per Box:** $20–$35 (Includes all overhead; excludes donated food value). * **Leverage Ratio:** 4:1 (Every $1 of municipal spend is paired with $4 of donated value from Harvesters and After the Harvest). * **Target Efficiency:** Benchmark validated against the MD Food Bank Mobile Market (4.2:1 ROI). --- ## 4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) for Renewal To secure Season 2 funding, StreetCart KC must hit the following 60-day targets: 1. **Unique Households Served:** 3,000–5,000 2. **Equity Concentration:** >70% of deliveries must terminate in priority ZIPs (64127, 64128, 64130, 64132, 66101, 66105). 3. **Volunteer Retention:** >40% of signups completing 3+ routes. 4. **Food Security Shift:** Measurable improvement on the USDA 6-item module among recipients. 5. **Cost Efficiency:** Fully loaded per-box cost remains under $35. --- ## 5. Strategic Advantage vs. Status Quo Traditional brick-and-mortar grocery subsidies often exceed $25M for a single point of failure. StreetCart KC provides a decentralized, partner-based network that scales with volunteer momentum rather than real estate costs. It converts the $0 fare-free streetcar corridor into a $500K logistics scoreboard.
