Feeding the Block
through the Kinetic Spine.

Converting Kansas City's fare-free streetcar corridor into a gamified food access backbone.

Layer 01

The Spine

The fare-free streetcar acts as the high-velocity distribution rail, moving fresh goods through the city's central corridor with zero-cost transit for the end-user.

Layer 02

The Tournament

Gamified food access brackets. Neighborhoods compete for 'Harvest Points' to unlock subsidized delivery windows and artisan market pop-ups.

Active Brackets: 12 Stations
Layer 03

The Last Mile

Localized hub-and-spoke delivery using electric micro-transit and community-led lockers at every streetcar stop to ensure food reaches the kitchen table.
