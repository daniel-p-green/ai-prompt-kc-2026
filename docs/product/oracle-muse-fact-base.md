## Locked Decision

### Primary thesis

StreetCart KC uses the Kansas City streetcar corridor as the public-facing spine for a 60-day food-access pilot, while pantry and nonprofit partners handle neighborhood fulfillment into the highest-need ZIP codes.

### Streetcar vs. bus line

Use this sentence everywhere:

> StreetCart KC uses the streetcar corridor to organize the city-facing program, while existing bus routes and partner agencies extend reach into the surrounding priority ZIP codes.

### Non-negotiable framing

- The streetcar is the public spine, not the full logistics system.
- Phase one does not depend on carrying food on the streetcar.
- Pickup is the default service motion. Delivery is selective.
- The bracket and scoreboard are the engagement engine, not the value proposition.
- The value proposition is verified household access in priority ZIP codes.
- The wrap concept is a renewal-phase reward, not a day-one dependency.
