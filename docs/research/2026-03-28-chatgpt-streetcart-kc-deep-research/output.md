## Curveballs, failure modes, and successful pivots

### Successful adaptation patterns seen in comparable programs

Transit-node distribution formats (bus stops, transit centers, rail stations) reduce ÔÇťsearch costsÔÇŁ for recipients by placing food at predictable, already-used mobility hubs. Engagement mechanics work best when they are simple and directly tied to measurable action.
