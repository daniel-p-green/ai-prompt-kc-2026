# Disco Cloud Celebration

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/disco-cloud/

#### Join us as we celebrate the 2023 Art in the Loop Streetcar “Disco Cloud” by Fred Trease.

The public is invited to attend the unveiling of KC Streetcar #804 for the Art in the Loop Project “Celebrate” on Wednesday, June 21 from 11:00 a.m. – 12:00 a.m. at the Union Station Streetcar Stop .

This will be a chance for Kansas City to meet local artist Fred Trease and see his artwork ‘ Disco Cloud ’ wrapped around a streetcar. This event will also feature a music performance from DJ and Producer Encode. Encode is a character, artist and a project created by Xavier Martin. This celebration is free and open to the public however guests can RSVO online: https://www.eventbrite.com/e/2023.com .
