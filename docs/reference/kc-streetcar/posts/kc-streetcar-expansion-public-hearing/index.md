# KC Streetcar Expansion Public Hearing

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/kc-streetcar-expansion-public-hearing/

#### Public Hearing for the “Kansas City Main Street Rail Transportation Development District”

On Thursday, September 15 starting at 9:00 am, the public is invited to attend a public hearing at the Jackson County Courthouse in downtown Kansas City. At that time, the public will be able to give testimony on the proposed KC Main Street Transportation Development District (TDD) thus leading to a KC Streetcar expansion plan.

The public may testify in support or opposition to the proposed Transportation Development District in the Division 6 courtroom of the Jackson County Courthouse, 415 East 12th Street, Kansas City, MO 64106.

For the official public notice, visit the Kansas City Regional Transit Alliance (KC-RTA) website: https://kcrta.org/streetcar
