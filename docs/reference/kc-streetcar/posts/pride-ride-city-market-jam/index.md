# Pride Ride + City Market Jam

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/pride-ride-city-market-jam/

The KC Streetcar and The City Market teamed up for Kansas City’s first ever Pride Ride & City Market Jam, a vibrant celebration of Kansas City’s LGBTQIA+ community. On Friday, June 21, from 5:30 – 8:30 p.m . the City Market was awash in music, dance, and jamming good times. This free, all-ages community celebration kicked off at the City Market Streetcar Stop at 5th & Walnut. Event Highlights: 5:30-6:30 p.m. – The festivities began with a dynamic performance by Sass-a-Brass , Kansas City’s street parade jazz band. The Pride Ride continued on board the KC Streetcar for a musical ride around the River Market, followed the parade to City Market for an exuberant celebration. 6:30-7:00 p.m. – At City Market Square, a lively swing dance performance by KC Swingout, Kansas City’s premier swing dance club, entertained the audience. 7:00-8:30 p.m . – The evening hit a high note with a live performance by local LGBTQIA+ band Jamogi & The Jammers . Their energy and sound united the crowd in celebration and joy. Throughout the Evening:

- Roller-skating demos from Sk8 Shot Studios .
- Merchandise available at the KC Current Teal Mobile.
- Raffle prizes and giveaways.
- Participants hung out on the patio of Enzo Bistro & Wine Bar for cocktails and bites
The Pride Ride & City Market Jam broadcast live on 90.9FM The Bridge and on The Bridge App, from the Enzo Bistro & Wine Patio. The Pride Ride & City Market Jam is made possible with support from KC Streetcar Authority, City Market, 90.9 The Bridge, Enzo Bistro & Wine Bar, and the River Market Community Association.
