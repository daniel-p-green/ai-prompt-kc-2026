# Singleton Yard Takes Form

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/singleton-yard-takes-form/

## Singleton Yard Gets Exterior Walls

Early Monday morning, a 133 foot crane arrived at the Singleton Yard to begin lifting and placing the precast exterior wall panels for the Singleton Yard. Holmes Street will be closed between 2nd and 3rd Streets for one week to allow room to safely operate the crane.
