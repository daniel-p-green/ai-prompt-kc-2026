# Trick-or-Treat!

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/trick-or-treat/

### The KC Streetcar is getting ready for Halloween.

The KC Streetcar is inviting trick-or-treaters of all ages to hop on board any streetcar on October 31, from 2:30 p.m. – 4:30 p.m., for some trick-or-treating.

This year we are proud to partner with Russell Stover Chocolates to bring this hometown candy to our riders. KC Streetcar team members will be on board every streetcar, giving out candy to riders. Costumes are a plus but not mandatory to participate.

WHAT : Trick-or-Treating on the KC Streetcar

WHEN : October 31, 2024, from 2:30 – 4:30 p.m.

WHERE : On board all streetcars

HOW : Hop on board any streetcar during this time to receive some Halloween treats
