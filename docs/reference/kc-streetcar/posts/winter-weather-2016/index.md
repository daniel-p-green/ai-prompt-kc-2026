# Winter Weather During Testing

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/winter-weather-2016/

The KC Streetcar is an all season vehicle and can operate in snowy conditions unless those conditions are unsafe. The City of Kansas City (Department of Public Works) is responsible for clearing any snow on the tracks and the roadway and perform those functions in line with their standard snow removal procedures. KC Streetcar Authority contractors are responsible for clearing all of the 16 streetcar platforms. Click here for a recent video of the KC Streetcar crews at work .

For more information about snowy conditions and streetcar travel, visit a KSHB-41 News for a recent story.
