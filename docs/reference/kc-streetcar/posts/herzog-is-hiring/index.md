# Be part of the KC Streetcar Team

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/herzog-is-hiring/

Herzog Transit Services (HTSI) is looking to add to its Kansas City Streetcar team. Herzog is the contractor responsible for the operations, maintenance and safety & security of the KC Streetcar system, reporting to the KC Streetcar Authority and the City of Kansas City, MO.
