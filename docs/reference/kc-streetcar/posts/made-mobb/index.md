# MADE MOBB x KC Streetcar

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/made-mobb/

MADE MOBB x KC STREETCAR SWAG (click here)

Nearly a decade after first teaming up to mark the launch of downtown’s KC Streetcar, two Kansas City originals — MADE MOBB and the KC Streetcar — are reuniting to celebrate the next chapter in Kansas City’s public transit story: the Grand Opening of the Main Street Extension. At the heart of this collaboration is a shared theme: “Connection.” From connecting people to places and experiences, to uniting neighborhoods and creative communities, the KC Streetcar and MADE MOBB share a common mission — to move Kansas City forward, together.

The MADE MOBB x KC Streetcar exclusive collection features:

- A limited-edition t-shirt celebrating the entire KC Streetcar route
- A KC Streetcar x MADE MOBB hat, and
- A custom tote bag — all designed with MADE MOBB’s signature streetwear style.
Items are available at MADE MOBB HQ and online at mademobb.com .

About MADE MOBB: MADE MOBB is a Kansas City streetwear brand rooted in community, creativity, and KC pride. Established in 2013, MADE MOBB designs apparel and experiences that celebrate Kansas City culture and the people who make it move.
