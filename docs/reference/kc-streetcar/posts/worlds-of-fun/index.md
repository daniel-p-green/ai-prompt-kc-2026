#### Kansas City to Celebrate the 50th Anniversary of Worlds of Fun in a Big Way

Worlds of Fun and the KC Streetcar are proud to partner on the latest streetcar wrap. Kansas City is invited to welcome the new Worlds of Fun Streetcar as it rolls into the Union Station streetcar stop escorted by Snoopy and Worlds of Fun ambassadors.

WHEN : Friday, March 31, 3:00 p.m. – 4:30 p.m.

WHERE : Union Station Streetcar Stop (30 W Pershing Road, Kansas City, MO 64108)

WHO : Snoopy, Worlds of Fun ambassadors & leadership, and the KC Streetcar team.

WHY : A special “zinger” of a streetcar wrap to commemorate the 50th Anniversary of Worlds of Fun, celebrate the return of the Zambezi Zinger this season, and kick off another fun-filled Worlds of Fun season. Worlds of Fun Opening Day is April 8.
