# Halloween on the KC Streetcar

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/halloween-kc-streetcar/

### Celebrate Halloween on board all of the KC Streetcars on Wednesday, October 31, from 11:30am-1:00pm.

### Together with Russell Stover, we will be distributing candy to all trick or treaters on board the streetcars.

If taking photos, share them and tag us (@kcstreetcar and @russellstover) on Twitter, Facebook and Instagram. Use hashtag #kcstreetcar and #makehappy

### Spooktacular Halloween Safety Rules

Weapons and flammable materials are not allowed on the KC Streetcar.

When getting on and off the KC Streetcar, please take care to keep costumes clear of the doors and tracks…and never cross in front of the streetcar.
