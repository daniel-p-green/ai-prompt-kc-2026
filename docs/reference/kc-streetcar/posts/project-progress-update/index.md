# Project Progress Update

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/project-progress-update/

The orange cones are starting to diminish up and down Main Street as significant progress is being made in the construction of the downtown KC Streetcar.

Approximately 96% of century old sewer and water lines along the alignment have been upgraded or replaced, totaling 32,687 linear feet of new and improved lines.

More than 15,430 feet of track is completely constructed from the Main Street Bridge to the River Market, with crews starting to work on track construction connecting to the Singleton Yard.
