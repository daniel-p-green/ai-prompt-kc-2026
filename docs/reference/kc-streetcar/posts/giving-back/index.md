## Streetcar Team To Build a Children’s Sensory Garden

Local Partnership to Benefit the Children’s Center Campus

Kansas City, Missouri – On Thursday, July 2, at 9am, join with the Children’s Center Campus (CCC) as they celebrate the start of construction of a Sensory Garden being built by the KC Streetcar Constructors, the team responsible for building the downtown streetcar. Members of the KC Streetcar Constructors team have volunteered time, supplies, equipment and manpower to build the garden that will benefit the Children’s Center for the Visually Impaired (CCVI), Children’s Therapeutic Learning Center (CLTC), and the YMCA Early Learning Center. The annual CCC Independence Day Student Parade will follow the event.

Special guests include newly re-elected Mayor Sly James, the students and families of CCC, CCC board members, and the KC Streetcar Constructors. The special event will take place at the CCC Campus located at 31st and Main Street.
