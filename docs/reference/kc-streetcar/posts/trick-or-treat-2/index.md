# Trick-Or-Treat

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/trick-or-treat-2/

### The KC Streetcar is getting ready for Halloween!🎃🎃

Trick-or-treaters of all ages are invited to hop on board the KC Streetcar and visit various stops for some halloween candy. As always, we are proud to partner with Russell Stover Chocolates to bring this hometown candy to our riders. KC Streetcar team members will be at the below stops giving out candy to riders. Costumes are a plus but not mandatory to participate.

WHAT : Trick-or-Treating along the newly expanded KC Streetcar route

WHEN : Friday, October 31, 2025, from 1:00 – 3:00 p.m.

WHERE : Trick-or-Treating at specific streetcar stops:

- UMKC
- Armour Northbound
- Union Hill Northbound
- Union Station Southbound
- Library Northbound
HOW : Hop on board and visit any of the above stops for some amazing Halloween treats
