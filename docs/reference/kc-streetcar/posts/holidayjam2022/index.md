# KC Streetcar Holiday Jam

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/holidayjam2022/

After two + long years, the KC Streetcar Holiday Jam is back with live music performances on board the Holiday Streetcar, at streetcar stops and in the City Market .

Head downtown this holiday season and enjoy some free live music on board the free KC Streetcar as you explore downtown from the City Market to Union Station. The Kansas City Streetcar is hosting live musical performances on board the Holiday Streetcar and at streetcar stops from Nov 30 – Dec. 17.

Music will range from traditional holiday music, covers and original music performed by local artists. Enjoy the holidays downtown and on board the KC Streetcar this season.

How to you know which streetcar is playing music? Well, that is part of the fun. All performances will be performed on board the Holiday Streetcar and start at the Union Station stop. Once we take off, you can hop on the Holiday Streetcar anywhere on the route during the scheduled performance time to enjoy the live music. Some performances are scheduled at streetcar stops and those locations are below. The best way to be in the know is to follow the KC Streetcar on Twitter , Facebook and Instagram .

The Holiday Streetcar is proudly sponsored by 91.9 FM Classical KC .

- Wednesday, Dec. 7, 4:00-5:30pm: Love x 2 on board the Holiday Streetcar (Trumpet + Melodica. Blues, Jazz, Holiday Tunes & Covers)
- Wednesday, Dec. 7, 5:30-7:00pm: Brooke Knoll on board the Holiday Streetcar (Classical + Contemporary Harp)
- Wednesday, Dec. 14, 5:30-7:00pm: C.T. Thongklin on board the Holiday Streetcar (Classical, Contemporary & Pop Violin)
- Wednesday, Dec. 14, 6:00-7:30pm: Heartsong (KC Women’s Chorus) at the Union Station Streetcar Stop (Vocal harmonies, Holiday Music)
- Saturday, Dec. 17, 10:00am-12:00pm: Volker Brass in the City Market (Brass Quintet. Classical & Holiday Music)
*Schedule is subject to change due to unforeseen circumstances. All attempts will be made to reschedule.
