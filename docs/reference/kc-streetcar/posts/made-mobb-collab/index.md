### MADE MOBB and KC Streetcar Reunite for Exclusive Collaboration Celebrating Main Street Extension Grand Opening

Nearly a decade after first teaming up to mark the launch of downtown’s KC Streetcar, two Kansas City originals — MADE MOBB and the KC Streetcar — are reuniting to celebrate the next chapter in Kansas City’s public transit story: the Grand Opening of the Main Street Extension.

Nine and a half years ago, the collaboration between MADE MOBB and KC Streetcar marked the beginning of a new era of connection in downtown Kansas City. Now, the partnership returns to honor the expansion of the Streetcar line south to the Country Club Plaza and UMKC, extending the system by 3.5 miles and reconnecting communities along Main Street.

At the heart of this collaboration is a shared theme: “Connection.” From connecting people to places and experiences, to uniting neighborhoods and creative communities, the KC Streetcar and MADE MOBB share a common mission — to move Kansas City forward, together.

The MADE MOBB x KC Streetcar exclusive collection features:

- A limited-edition t-shirt celebrating the entire KC Streetcar route
- A KC Streetcar x MADE MOBB hat, and
- A custom tote bag — all designed with MADE MOBB’s signature streetwear style.
