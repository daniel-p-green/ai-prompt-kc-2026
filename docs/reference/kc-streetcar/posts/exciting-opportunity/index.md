## KCSA issues Request for Proposal for Website Design & Development.

The Kansas City Streetcar Authority (KCSA) is issuing this Request for Proposal (RFP) to firms or individuals interested in providing website design and development services (Bidders) to support design, development and launch of KCSA’s “Ride KC Streetcar” website. Proposals are due to the KC Streetcar Authority by June 30, 2015

Questions can be directed to Ebony Jackson with the KC Streetcar Authority at ejackson@kcstreetcar.org or 816.859.7038.
