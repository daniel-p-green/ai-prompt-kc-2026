# KC Streetcar In Production

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/kc-streetcar-in-production/

## Vehicle # 1 in dynamic testing

At the September 2015 meeting of the KC Streetcar Authority (KCSA), Tom Gerend, executive director of the KCSA, updated board members on the manufacturing progress of the KC Streetcar vehicle. Vehicle number one is has entered the dynamic testing phase and is on the test track this week in the CAF facility in Elmira, NY.
