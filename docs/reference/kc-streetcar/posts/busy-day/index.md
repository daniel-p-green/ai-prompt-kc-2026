Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/busy-day/

## The work day starts early on the KC Streetcar

The work day starts early on the downtown KC Streetcar, which stretches from the River Market at 3rd and Delaware to the Main Street Bridge near Union Station. Since construction started last year, progress has been made on the third party utility relocations, water and sewer upgrades, WiFi conduit installation, and track construction.

It takes many disciplines and hard work from a massive amount of people to get the job done. No matter if it is a snowy winter morning or a blazing August afternoon, it always starts with worksite prep work. Take a look at a Busy Morning on the KC Streetcar .
