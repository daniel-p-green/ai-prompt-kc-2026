# 816 Day 2023

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/816-day-2023/

### WHAT IS 816 DAY?

816 Day is the annual, city-proclaimed celebration of Kansas City culture, featuring live entertainment, branded events, and fun activities. On this day, community connectedness is strengthened by local business and resident pride.

### WHAT’S NEW THIS YEAR?

Since 2018, the downtown community has been celebrating all things 816! This year we are partnering with Art in the Loop , the Downtown Council , Downtown Neighborhood Association , the River Market Community Association , and the Power & Light District to bring a celebration worthy of our area code to the Historic City Market . Between 5:30 – 8:00 p.m., the downtown fun will be at City Market with three Art in the Loop performances featuring:

- Traditional Music Society: Soundz of Samba: Take a musical excursion that will engulf you in the biggest parade in the entire world – Carnival of Rio de Janeiro! Bass drums, bells, shakers, snares, and whistles all recreate the national rhythm and dance of Brazil—Samba.
- Circus Scorpius: A dynamic display of breathtaking physical acrobatics.
- Underground Homies: Artists Steddy P (born and raised in KC) and GIPSON (a 5 year transplant) that focuses on being “Made in KC” as people.

### LET’S CELEBRATE 816 DAY STARTING FIRST FRIDAY !

Celebrate 816 Day between August 4 – 16, 2023 with a friendly game of Scavenger Hunt Bingo. Discover new destinations and revisit old stomping grounds around downtown Kansas City and along the KC Streetcar route. Complete your Bingo card by snapping a QR Code at a downtown artwork, landmark and on board the KC Streetcar, or picking up a sticker at the select downtown businesses, coffee shops, and restaurants. * Please help support our local businesses by purchasing an item to get an 816 Day Bingo sticker.
