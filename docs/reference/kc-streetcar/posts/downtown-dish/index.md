# Downtown Dish

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/downtown-dish/

The Downtown Dish is your virtual opportunity to sit down with Downtown KC chefs, bartenders, baristas, retailers, and artists each week.

It serves up what’s new, in the works, and on the menu in the re-emerging Downtown dining, shopping, and entertainment scenes. It’s all in the name of supporting businesses that make Downtown KC the vibrant heart of our city.

And NOW the Downtown Dish is an audio podcast and part of the Pitch Podcast Network. You can tune in every week from wherever you listen to your favorite podcasts. Click here to access the Downtown Dish as an audio podcast: https://www.thepitchkc.com/podcasts/ .

Check out the full rundown of all Downtown Dish episodes here: https://www.downtownkc.org/downtown-dish /

The Downtown Dish is a collaboration with the KC Streetcar, Downtown Council, the Downtown Neighborhood Association , Lynchpin Ideas, and the downtown districts. This weekly talk show is hosted by the fabulous Katy Schamberger @katywrites .

Subscribe to the Downtown Dish YouTube channel to be notified of new episodes as well as catching past episodes.

## Downtown Dish: Prep for the AFC ChampionShip [Feb. 4, 2021]

This week, Downtown Dish host Katy Schamberger catches up with Trish Mitchell. Trish, a multi-talented entrepreneur, has added new titles to her resume: magazine founder and editor-in-chief! Trish gives us the scoop on her new publication, All Black Everything Magazine. Find out what’s inside the issue, where you can get a copy and what’s coming up later this year.
