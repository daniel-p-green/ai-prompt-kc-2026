# Open House Meeting

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/open-house-meeting/

Main St Extension Public Meeting Set For March 30, 5-7pm.

The KC Streetcar Main Street Extension is preparing to break ground! Join with the Kansas City Streetcar Authority, KC Streetcar Constructors , City of Kansas City, Missouri , and Kansas City Area Transportation Authority at a project open house meeting on Wednesday, March 30 from 5-7pm.

Stop by in-person to view current renderings, learn about upcoming construction activities and schedule, and talk to project team members to get your questions answered. No formal presentation will be given.

WHEN : 5-7 p.m. on Wednesday, March 30, 2022

WHERE : Drexel Hall, 3301 Baltimore Ave., Kansas City, MO 64111

WHAT : An in-person open house for the public to learn more about the KC Streetcar Main Street Extension prior to groundbreaking. If you are unable to attend, meeting materials will be posted on the project website, www.buildkcstreetcar.com .

Official groundbreaking for the KC Streetcar Main Street Extension is anticipated in early April 2022 . The construction of the project is estimated to take two and a half years with passengers ready to ride in 2025. The KC Streetcar Main Street Extension is being built by the KC Streetcar Constructors (KCSC). KCSC is a joint venture between Herzog Contracting Corp. and Stacy and Witbeck, and is joined by Burns & McDonnell and JE Dunn Construction to form the initial construction team. The project is overseen by the joint partnership of the City of Kansas City, Missouri, Kansas City Area Transportation Authority and the KC Streetcar Authority.

For more information about the KC Streetcar Main Street Extension construction, please visit: www.buildkcstreetcar.com or contact the KC Streetcar Constructors at info@buildkcstreetcar.com .
