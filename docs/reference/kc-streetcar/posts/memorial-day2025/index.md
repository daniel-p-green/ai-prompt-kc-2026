# Memorial Day 2025

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/memorial-day2025/

The KC Streetcar is the best late night ride in Downtown KC. Heading to Celebration at the Station? The KC Streetcar can get you to and from the fun. We are operating four streetcars every day, with an added 5th streetcar for Sunday evening, May 25. The hours of operation for Memorial Day Weekend are:

- Friday, May 23: 6:00 a.m. – 1:00 a.m.
- Saturday, May 24: 7:00 a.m. – 1:00 a.m.
- Sunday, May 25: 7:00 a.m. – Midnight
- Monday, May 26: 6:00 a.m. – Midnight
