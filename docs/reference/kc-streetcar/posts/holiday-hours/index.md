# Holiday Hours

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/holiday-hours/

#### The KC Streetcar will operate with the following Holiday Hours:

#### Saturday, December 24: 7:00 a.m. – 10:00 p.m.

#### Sunday, December 25: 8:00 a.m. – 8:00 p.m. (Christmas Day)

#### Monday, December 26: 7:00 a.m. – 10:00 p.m. (one hour late start & end)

#### Saturday, December 31: 7:00 a.m. – 1:00 a.m. (regular service hours)

#### Sunday, January 1, 2023: 8:00 a.m. – 10:00 p.m. (New Year’s Day)

The Administrative office of the KC Streetcar will be closed during the above dates as well as January 2, 2023.
