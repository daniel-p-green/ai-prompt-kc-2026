# Halloween Safety Reminders

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/halloween-safety-reminders/

This is the first halloween season for the KC Streetcar. Even during these fun times, it’s important to Be Smart, Be Safe, and Be Ready.

KC Streetcar hours of operation for this weekend and Halloween are as follows:

- Friday, October 28: 6:00am – 2:00am
- Saturday, October 29: 7:00am – 2:00am
- Sunday, October 30: 7:00am – 10:00pm
- Monday, October 31: 6:00am – Midnight
When riding the KC Streetcar, please keep in mind:

- Facial coverings, including Halloween masks and hoods covering the face, are not permitted on the KC Streetcar due to safety and security concerns. The only exceptions are small children and those observing religious customs.
- Weapons and flammable materials are not allowed on the KC Streetcar.
- When getting on and off the KC Streetcar, please take care to keep costumes clear of the doors and tracks…and never cross in front of the streetcar.
