# Visit Downtown This Holiday Season

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/visit-downtown-this-holiday-season/

Downtown restaurants along the streetcar route will be Open for Business over the Christmas holiday. This is your perfect chance to make a reservation for Christmas Eve or Christmas Day.

The Downtown Council surveyed restaurants along the streetcar route and learned that you have several dining options on Wednesday, Christmas Eve or Thursday, Christmas Day.

For more information, visit the Downtown KC Website .
