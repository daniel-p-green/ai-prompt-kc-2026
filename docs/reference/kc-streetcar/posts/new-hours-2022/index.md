# New Hours

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/new-hours-2022/

#### Starting February 28, 2022, the KC Streetcar will increase hours of service for both weekdays and weekends.

This change in hours is in direct response to downtown activities and the increase in KC Streetcar ridership experienced in recent months.

The adjusted hours of operations are:

#### Monday – Thursday: 6:00 a.m. – 11:00 p.m.

#### Friday: 6:00 a.m. – 1:00 a.m.

#### Saturday: 7:00 a.m. – 1:00 a.m.

#### Sunday: 7:00 a.m. – 11:00 p.m.
