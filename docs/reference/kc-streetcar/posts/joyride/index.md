# Interplanetary Joyride

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/joyride/

## Take a WONDERful Interplanetary Joyride on the KC Streetcar

#### Special Art in the Loop Performance Onboard the KC Streetcar

Take an Interplanetary Joyride full of music, art, and poetry. Hop onboard KC Streetcar #814 on July 16 to enjoy Tres Collective , a collective of renowned spoken word artists, authors, and creatives. Immediately following riders will experience Danielle Ate the Sandwich perform as her original comedy character “Jer,” a singer/songwriter best known for his children’s music. Tres Collective will perform first between 6:00-7:00 p.m., followed by “Jer” from 7:00-8:00 p.m. All performances will take place on KC Streetcar #814 (the purple one).

This event is free and open to the public. Just hop on the Interplanetary Joyride KC Streetcar #814 from any streetcar stop on Wednesday, July 16, between 6:00-8:00 p.m. You can follow along on the KC Streetcar Instagram as well www.instagram.com/kcstreetcar .

This year marks the 12th year of the Art in the Loop Project. The Art in the Loop project is a curated outdoor exhibition of temporary artworks in the heart of downtown. The artworks are both visual and performance based, spanning from May through October.

Visit artintheloop.com for more information about the 2025 Art in the Loop Project: Wonder.
