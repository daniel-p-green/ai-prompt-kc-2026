# #KCStreetcar Lunch Bunch

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/kcstreetcar-lunch-bunch-2/

## Join with Downtown’s Lunch Bunch every Wednesday, from 11:30am – 1:00pm, to help spread the love to our downtown restaurants during streetcar construction

#KCStreetcar and Lunch Bunch are part of the Open for Business campaign that’s designed to promote and benefit restaurants and retailers on the Downtown streetcar line during streetcar construction. The two-mile streetcar route runs along Main Street from Pershing Road (Union Station) north to 5th Street (River Market). The route circles the River Market area and then returns to Main Street and travels south to Union Station.

The “Lunch Bunch” crowd gathers each week at a different district of Downtown. Over the last month, the Lunch Bunch has dined in the Crossroads, River Market and Power & Light District. Each week is different and the notices for each outing can be found on our Facebook and Twitter feeds.

To get involved and learn more about the Lunch Bunch, visit the VisitKC website .
