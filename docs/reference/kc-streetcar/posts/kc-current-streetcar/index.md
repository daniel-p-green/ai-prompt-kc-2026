### Kansas City Current and KC Streetcar Launch New Co-Branded Streetcar

The Kansas City Current and the KC Streetcar Authority are proud to announce a new streetcar wrap design as the Current gets ready to begin the 2023 season. The official reveal of the KC Current Streetcar “805” will take place on Friday, March 24 at 2:00 p.m. at the Union Station Streetcar Stop. Current players Desiree Scott (Midfielder) and Vanessa DiBernardo (Midfielder) will also be in attendance.

In a few years, the Kansas City Streetcar will serve the new KC Current Stadium when the KC Streetcar Riverfront Extension is complete in 2025. This will directly link Berkley Riverfront Park with downtown, midtown and the Country Club Plaza, increasing rider connectivity to businesses, residences, schools, cultural institutions, and more.
