# July 4th Holiday Hours

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/july-4-2025/

### The KC Streetcar will operate with the following hours and service levels during the July 4th holiday and weekend:

- Thursday, July 3: 6:00 a.m. to Midnight (4 streetcar service 10:00 a.m. – 6:00 p.m.)
- Friday, July 4: 7:00 a.m. to 1:00 a.m. (4 streetcar service 10:00 a.m. – 10:00 p.m.)
- Saturday, July 5: 7:00 a.m. to 1:00 a.m. (4 streetcar service 10:00 a.m. – 8:00 p.m.)
- Sunday, July 6: 7:00 a.m. to Midnight (4 streetcar service 10:00 a.m. – 6:00 p.m.)
