# Kansas City Marathon

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/kc-marathon/

The Kansas City Marathon is scheduled for Saturday, October 21, 2017, and the KC Streetcar is THE best way to get to the starting line at Crown Center. Don’t hassle with parking, leave the driving to us.

The streetcar will begin running at 5:30 a.m. on the morning of the race. Hop on at any stop and ride the streetcar to the Union Station stop, just one block from the start line. Free parking lots on Saturdays at the North loop at 7th and Main Street.

For more information about the KC Marathon and the race, click here https://www.sportkc.org/marathon/ .

For information about directions, click here https://www.sportkc.org/marathon/guide-to-kcm/directionsparking/ .
