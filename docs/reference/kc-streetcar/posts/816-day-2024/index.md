# 816 Day 2024

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/816-day-2024/

### WHAT IS 816 DAY?

816 Day is the annual, city-proclaimed celebration of Kansas City culture, featuring live entertainment, branded events, and fun activities. On this day, community connectedness is strengthened by local business and resident pride.

### LET’S CELEBRATE 816 DAY WITH BINGO!

Celebrate 816 Day between August 2 – 16, 2024 with a friendly game of Scavenger Hunt Bingo. Discover new destinations and revisit old stomping grounds around downtown Kansas City and along the KC Streetcar route. Complete your Bingo card by snapping a QR Code at a downtown artwork, landmark and on board the KC Streetcar, or picking up a sticker at the select downtown businesses, coffee shops, and restaurants. *Purchase at participating businesses is not required to play 816 Day Bingo.

### WHAT’S NEW THIS YEAR?

Since 2018, the downtown community has been celebrating all things 816! As always, we are partnering with Art in the Loop , the Downtown Council , Downtown Neighborhood Association , the River Market Community Association , and the Power & Light District to bring a celebration worthy of our area code to the Historic City Market . Between 5:30 – 8:00 p.m., on Friday, August 16, the downtown fun will be at City Market with three Art in the Loop performances featuring:

Danielle Ate The Sandwich: A delightful performance of original folk songs performed on ukulele and guitar. The performance will explore Convivencia through the conflict and harmony of how we live through the human experience.

45 Grados Flamenco featuring José Faus : Music, Poetry, and Dance come together in this bi-lingual performance that explores different cultures that co-exist in music, rhythm, songs, and spoken word.

Kadesh Flow : Kadesh Flow’s wide reaching content hops from socioeconomic musings to life bars that are laced with anime and video game references. His performance will be alongside his “Deshtet”, exploring differing ideas, blending them, generating conflict, and resolving tension.
