# KC Streetcar Holiday Jam 2023

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/holiday-jam-2023/

### Who’s ready to jam this holiday season?

Head downtown this holiday season and enjoy free live music on board the free KC Streetcar as you explore downtown from the City Market to Union Station. The Kansas City Streetcar is hosting live musical performances on board the Holiday Streetcar during Saturday afternoons and Wednesday evenings from Nov 25 – Dec. 16.

Music will range from traditional holiday music, covers and original music performed by local artists. Enjoy the holidays downtown and on board the KC Streetcar this season.

How to you know which streetcar is playing music? Well, that is part of the fun. All performances will be performed on board the Holiday Streetcar #801 and start at the Union Station stop. Once we take off, you can hop on the Holiday Streetcar #801 anywhere on the route during the scheduled performance time to enjoy the live music. The best way to be in the know is to follow the KC Streetcar on X , Facebook and Instagram .
