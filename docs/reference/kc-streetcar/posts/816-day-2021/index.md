# 816 Day 2021

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/816-day-2021/

WHAT IS 816 DAY? In 2018, KC City Council passed a resolution making August 16 Kansas City’s newest holiday. It’s that special day in August when the date and our area code coincide and is a celebration of all things KC.

LET’S CELEBRATE Celebrate 816 Day between August 9 – 16, 2021 with a friendly game of Bingo.
