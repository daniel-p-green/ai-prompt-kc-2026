### KC Streetcar Riverfront Extension Marks 100% Track Completion

#### Community Celebration Set for April 22

The KC Streetcar Riverfront Extension construction crews will reach a major milestone next week with the completion of 100 percent of the track work, just 14 months after the project broke ground.

To mark this exciting achievement, the community is invited to join with our federal partners with the Federal Transit Administration at an open-house style celebration at Berkley Riverfront.

WHEN : 3 p.m. on Tuesday, April 22, 2025

WHERE : The future streetcar stop near Riverfront Drive and Berkley Plaza. (Rain backup location at PortKC offices, 110 Berkley Plaza Kansas City, MO 64120)

WHAT : A community celebration of track completion including opportunities to:

- Be one of the first to stand on the newly installed Riverfront Streetcar Stop
- Snap a pic with the KC Streetcar-themed photo booth
- Grab a goody bag featuring treats from Chinatown Market
- Enjoy snacks and NA beverages from featured area businesses
- Have a chance to be featured on the KC Streetcar Riverfront Extension Tiny Mic series!
MEDIA AVAILABILITY: Representatives of the project and construction teams will be available for interviews. No formal remarks are planned for this event.

For more information about the KC Streetcar Riverfront Extension construction, please visit http://riverfrontkcstreetcar.com or find the team online on X (@kcriverfront), Instagram (@kcriverfront), and Facebook (@kcstreetcarriverfront). Contact the construction team by email at info@riverfrontkcstreetcar.com or phone at 816-614-6826.
