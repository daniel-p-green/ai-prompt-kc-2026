# Thanksgiving 2023

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/thanksgiving-2023/

### The KC Streetcar will operate with the following hours during the Thanksgiving Holiday:

#### Thursday, November 23: 7:00 a.m. – 10:00 p.m.*

#### Friday, November 24: 6:00 a.m. – 1:00 a.m.

#### Saturday, November 25: 7:00 a.m. – 1:00 a.m.

#### Sunday, November 26: 7:00 a.m. – 12:00 a.m.

* Reflects a 1 hour delayed start and a 2 hour early end.
