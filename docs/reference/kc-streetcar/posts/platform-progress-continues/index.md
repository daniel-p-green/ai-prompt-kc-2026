# Platform Progress Continues

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/platform-progress-continues/

## Northbound track platform at 16th & Main

Steady progress on the route’s second streetcar stop to be constructed, the northbound platform at 16th and Main. This week crews are forming and pouring the seat wall, which is the back structure of the station stop.
