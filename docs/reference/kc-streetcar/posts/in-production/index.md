# In Production

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/in-production/

## The vehicle pieces are coming together as seen in Elmira, NY’s production facility.

The KC Streetcar is a modern, bi-directional, electrically-propelled streetcar. It consists of three car bodies joined with two articulation sections to form a single operating unit.

The KC Streetcar is a 100% low floor vehicle, which eliminates any barriers to passenger access within the vehicle. It also uses a spring over hydraulic system to provide ADA-compliant level boarding.
