# Cold Temps

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/cold-temps/

The KC Streetcar operates in all types of weather including the summer heat and the frigid cold winter.

As Kansas City braces for the Polar Vortex on January 30-31, 2019, the KC Streetcar will operate will regular hours of 6:00am – Midnight. The End of the Line de-boarding policy will also be lifted as along as riders adhere to the KC Streetcar rider Code of Conduct .

For cold weather tips, visit the City of Kansas City’s website .

Take care, Kansas City, and stay warm.
