# Labor Day 2025

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/labor-day-2025/

Celebrate the end of summer with the KC Streetcar. The KC Streetcar is the best way to get to the Chalk & Walk Fest in the City Market, KC Irish Fest at Crown Center, and everything else downtown!

- Monday, September 1: 7:00am – Midnight
