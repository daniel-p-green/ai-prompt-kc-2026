# Rail Rally!

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/rail-rally/

## Celebrate Track Construction Completion in the River Market and other Streetcar Milestones!

The City of Kansas City, Missouri, the Kansas City Streetcar Authority and the KC Streetcar Constructors invite future streetcar riders to celebrate the completion of streetcar track construction around the City Market at a special River Market Rail Rally on July 29 from 5 p.m. – 7 p.m.

The River Market Rail Rally will take place along 5 th Street between Main & Walnut Streets. Guests will also be the first to view the City Market streetcar station and art installation. The event will feature food, drink and live entertainment.

What : River Market Rail Rally

When : July 29, 2015 from 5 p.m. to 7 p.m.

Where : Fifth Street, between Main St. and Walnut St.

Who : Remarks from Mayor Sly James and Tom Gerend, Executive Director for the Kansas City Streetcar Authority
