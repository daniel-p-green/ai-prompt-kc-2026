## Navigating the work zone

As private utility work begins to wrap up near 10th and Main, and water improvements continue, navigating the 10th and Main work zones can be tricky.

Here is a m ap that may benefit you and your tenants/properties/employees, etc. as you maneuver the work zones.

Starting Monday, Jan. 19, 10th St will close between Baltimore – Main St because of continued private utility work. The KCATA bus movements in and out of the Transit Center will change, as noted in the map. Additionally, 9th St will close between Main and Walnut due to water work.

This work zone and traffic pattern will be in place through mid-February. The KC Streetcar team posts continual updates on our Facebook and Twitter feeds as well as this website.
