## Getting around the southbound track work in the CBD

Below is a map of what you can expect when navigating the Central Business District the week of May 4, 2015. All work is weather dependent and construction schedules can change from day-to-day.Roadway paving started the week of May 4 from 7th – 8th Streets and track forming and building continues from 9th – 10th Streets. Rebar mats are being laid from 10th Street to Petticoat Lane while platform electrical and WiFi conduits are going in from Petticoat to 12th Street.

During work, various side streets will be temporarily closed, and parking lots and garages will experience driveway closures or shifts.
