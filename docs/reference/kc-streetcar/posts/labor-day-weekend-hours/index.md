# Labor Day Weekend Hours

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/labor-day-weekend-hours/

The KC Streetcar will follow the below schedule for the Labor Day Holiday Weekend:

#### Friday, September 2: 6am-2am

#### Saturday, September 3: 7am-2am

#### Sunday, September 4: 7am-Midnight

#### Monday, September 5: 7am-10pm

If you are heading to Kansas City Irish Fest at Crown Center, we encourage people to be safe and take the streetcar to the Union Station stop and walk over to Crown Center. More information about Kansas City Irish Fest can be found on their website .
