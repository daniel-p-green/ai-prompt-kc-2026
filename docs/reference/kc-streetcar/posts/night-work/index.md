# Night Work

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/night-work/

## 3/30 – 4/3: Night work at 10th and Main, 7pm-5am.

During the night shift, 7pm-5am, Main at 10th St. traffic will be flagged with one lane in each direction. The previously implemented night work noise policy (no loud breaking after 10:30pm) will be in effect.

In other 10th and Main Work:

Starting Monday, March 30, crews will be working on the water mains and lines at 10th and Main St. for approximately two weeks. Please note that when traveling south on Main St, you will not be able to go east (left hand turn) onto 10th St.

In order to access 10th St east of Main, traffic can travel east on 10th St. from Baltimore in order to get through the 10th and Main intersection. We also anticipate traffic will be able to make an eastbound turn (right hand turn) from northbound Main St. onto 10th St.

In an effort to mitigate congestion during this work, the Metro buses will not be running on Petticoat or on Main St. between the Transit Center and 11th during this work.
