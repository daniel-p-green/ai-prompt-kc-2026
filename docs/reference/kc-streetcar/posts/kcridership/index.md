# Ridership

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/kcridership/

### Kansas City is riding the rails throughout the heart of downtown.

#### From May 6, opening day, until July 5, the streetcar has carried 381,914 people along the route.

#### During the month of June, 182,248 people rode the streetcar.

#### On the July 4th weekend, 30,391 people rode the streetcar.

#### As of this published date, the KC Streetcar has a daily average ridership of 6,365.

The weekends are popular times for families, visitors, residents and downtown explorers. That, coupled with all the events in and around downtown Kansas City, there is much activity in the heart of our city.
