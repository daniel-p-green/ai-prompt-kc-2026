# Let’s Go Chiefs!

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/lets-go-chiefs/

#### Cheer on the Super Bowl Bound Kansas City Chiefs THIS Friday on board the KC Streetcar

On Friday, January 31, from 5:30 p.m. – 7:00 p.m ., all of Chiefs Kingdom is invited to a special Super Bowl Red Friday Ride on the Chiefs Streetcar. Hop on with KC Wolf and the Rumble Drumline as we show off our Chiefs Pride before Super Bowl LIV on Sunday, February 2. Riders will have a chance to win prizes throughout the ride.

We’re gearing up to take part in the biggest RED FRIDAY in 50 years. There is no better place to take part in a Pre-Super Bowl celebration than at Union Station with free photos and your friends. McDonald’s will also be there with FREE sandwiches, snacks and giveaways.

Starting at 4:00 p.m., the Arrowstream Photo Bus will be at Union Station. Kansas Citians have been a dedicated fan base all season long, and so has The Photo Bus – being stationed in the Ford Tailgate for every home game in our Arrowstream. Come join us in supporting our Chiefs by grabbing some photos and sharing your Kingdom pride.

The Red Friday Ride celebration will consist of 1 ½ loops on board the Chiefs Streetcar, starting at 5:30 p.m. at the Union Station streetcar stop and ending at the Power & Light northbound streetcar stop at 14th & Main. From there, KC Wolf and the Rumble will exit the Chiefs Streetcar and take fans through the Power & Light District. There the party will continue in the KC Live! Block with the Red Kingdom Rally hosted by Sports Radio 810 WHB and featuring a special performance by Tech N9ne . All events are family friendly but space on the Chiefs Streetcar is limited.

Track the exact location of the Chiefs Streetcar, otherwise known as Streetcar 805, using the free City Post tracker for smart phones or our online tracker on the KC Streetcar website: https://kcstreetcar.org/route/streetcar-tracker/.

For KC Streetcar media questions, please contact Donna Mandelbaum with the KC Streetcar Authority at 816.627.2526 (office), 816.877.3219 (cell) or email at dmandelbaum@kcstreetcar.org .
