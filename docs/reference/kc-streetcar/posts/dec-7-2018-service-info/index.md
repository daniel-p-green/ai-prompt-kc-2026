# Dec. 7, 2018 Service Info

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/dec-7-2018-service-info/

The KC Streetcar will operate with regular Friday hours of 6:00am – 2:00am on Friday, December 7, 2018.

The planned visit by the President of the United States should not impact KC Streetcar travel or schedule.

For up to date information, please sign up for service text alerts by texting “kcstreetcar” to 797979 and follow us on Twitter, Facebook and Instagram.
