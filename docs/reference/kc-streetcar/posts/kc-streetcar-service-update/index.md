# KC Streetcar Service Update

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/kc-streetcar-service-update/

After the conclusion of streetcar service on Sunday, July 16, the KC Streetcar will undergo scheduled maintenance activity on the route. This is expected maintenance activity involving the re-tensioning of overhead wires. Work will take place on the west side of Main Street from Truman Road south to Union Station. Work will only affect the wires on the southbound portion of the streetcar route and there will be temporary lane closures during overnight hours while crews are working.

Depending on when work is completed, early morning Monday streetcar service may be affected. If that is the case, service will be supplemented with free Ride KC “streetcar link” buses until the work is complete.

Work is weather dependent and may change if determined the weather conditions are not favorable.

Updates will be made via our text alert service as well as through streetcar social media channels. To sign up to receive KC Streetcar service related text messages, text “KCSTREETCAR” to 797979.
