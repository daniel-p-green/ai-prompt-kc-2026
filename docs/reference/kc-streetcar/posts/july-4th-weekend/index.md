# July 4th Holiday Weekend

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/july-4th-weekend/

The KC Streetcar will operate with the following hours for the July 4th Holiday Weekend.

Friday, July 1: 6:00 a.m. – 1:00 a.m.

Saturday, July 2: 7:00 a.m. – 1:00 a.m.

Sunday, July 3: 7:00 a.m. – 1:00 a.m. (extended service by two hours)

Monday, July 4: 7:00 a.m. – 11:00 p.m. (delayed start by one hour)
