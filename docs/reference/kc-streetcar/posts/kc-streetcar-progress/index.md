# KC Streetcar Progress

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/kc-streetcar-progress/

## Utility work is 99% complete as of early summer

Even with the rainy forecasts, construction on the KC Downtown Streetcar has made significant progress this summer, with overhead wires being installed and the last of the track pours scheduled for this month.

Southbound track is fully connected from 3rd St in the River Market to 20th St in the Crossroads Art District. Northbound track is nearly done as crews work to complete the last track section in the Central Business District.

As of July 1, 2015:

- 95% of track is fully constructed (20,360 feet complete out of 21,771 total feet).
- 99% of water and sewer replacements and upgrades is complete.
- 82% of work on the Singleton Yard vehicle maintenance facility is complete.
