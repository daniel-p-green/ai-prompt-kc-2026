# KC2026 + KC Streetcar Suit Up

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/kc2026/

### KC2026 and KC Streetcar Bring FIFA World Cup 26™ Excitement to the Tracks Ahead of the Final Draw

KC2026 and the KC Streetcar are excited to announce the unveiling of two specially-wrapped KC Streetcars celebrating Kansas City’s role as a FIFA World Cup 26™ Host City. Debuting ahead of the highly anticipated FIFA World Cup 26™ Final Draw on December 5, these streetcar wraps are designed to elevate excitement across the city and showcase Kansas City’s pride to residents, visitors and global audiences.

“The KC Streetcar is an important partner as we plan to host and move residents and visitors next summer, so we’re thrilled that these branded streetcars are part of our early marketing efforts,” said Pam Kramer, Chief Executive Officer of KC2026. “As the world turns its attention to the Final Draw and the Kansas City region, these streetcars are a visible reminder of our enthusiasm to welcome the global soccer community.”

The two streetcars, each featuring identical designs, highlight the bold FIFA World Cup 26™ Kansas City Host City brand designs, including a nod to Kansas City’s iconic fountains, and recognition of its five Official Supporters of the Kansas City World Cup 2026 Host City. These streetcars will be in service from the River Market to UMKC through July 2026, carrying the energy and momentum of the world’s largest sporting competition along the entire route.

“These wrapped streetcars not only celebrate our city’s global moment but also embody the movement, connection, and vibrancy the KC Streetcar brings to Kansas City every day,” said Tom Gerend, Executive Director, KC Streetcar Authority.

Stay tuned for additional promotions and activations from KC Streetcar and KC2026 as Kansas City prepares to welcome fans from around the world next year. Visit https://kansascityfwc26.com/ for more KC2026 information.

#### About KC2026

A nonprofit organization overseeing strategy and delivering Host City duties for the FIFA World Cup 26™, KC2026 will plan, construct and implement all aspects of Kansas City’s largest sports and fan engagement event in the region’s history. KC2026 planning efforts are supported by its Official Kansas City World Cup 2026 Host City Supporters: Black & Veatch, JE Dunn Construction, Purina PetCare, Populous and The University of Kansas Health System. These global and regional companies are leaders in their respective industries and represent key sectors of KC’s economy. Host City Supporters are civic and brand partners who are helping bring the FIFA World Cup 26™ to life in Kansas City. Learn more at KansasCityFWC26.com.

#### About the Final Draw

The Final Draw will capture the world’s attention, bringing together FIFA officials, team ambassadors, delegates from all Host Cities and international media as the 48 qualified teams are placed into 12 groups of four. Kansas City is set to host four Group Stage matches (June 16, 20, 25 and 27, 2026), a Round of 32 match (July 3, 2026), and a Quarter-Final match (July 11, 2026) at Kansas City Stadium (Arrowhead). While the team groupings will be known on December 5, the final match schedule assigning specific Group Stage matches to host stadiums will follow a separate process by FIFA™.
