# Lost and Found

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/lost-and-found/

### It happens. And you’re not the first.

You’re shopping at the Plaza. You’re carrying bags from Science City, maybe a new outfit from the Westport, eclectics from the City Market.

You hop on the KC Streetcar to get to your next destination. You start striking up conversations with fellow passengers. Your stop approaches and you head on your way out the streetcar doors.

### Find your belongings.

No need to worry, once at the UMKC stop, the streetcar operators perform a sweep of the streetcars and pick up anything left behind. These items are then brought to the Singleton Yard after the end of their shift and logged into the Lost and Found.

So if you think you’ve lost something on board the streetcar, it may be at our facility right now. Give us a call at 816.627.2527 or email info@kcstreetcar.org. We are located at 600 East 3rd Street just east of the River Market area. Lost and Found calls will be answered Monday – Friday, 9:00am-4:30pm.

Please note: Onboard KC Streetcar video footage is not available for the purpose of locating lost or misplaced items. While cameras are used to support the safety and security of riders and operations, recorded footage cannot be reviewed or released for lost and found searches. Riders who believe they may have left an item on the streetcar are encouraged to submit a lost and found inquiry and check with KC Streetcar staff.
