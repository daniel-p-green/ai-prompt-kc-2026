# KC Streetcar Holiday Jam

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/holidayjam2025/

### Who’s ready to jam this holiday season?

Ride the KC Streetcar this holiday season and enjoy free live music on board the KC Streetcar and at streetcar stops. The Kansas City Streetcar is hosting live musical performances for the holidays on November 29, December 2, December 9, and December 16. Music will range from traditional holiday music, covers and original music performed by local artists.

How to you know which streetcar is playing music? Well, that is part of the fun. All on board performances will start at the UMKC streetcar stop. Once we take off, you can hop on the Holiday Jam Streetcar #809 anywhere on the route during the scheduled performance time to enjoy the live music. You can track the streetcar using the Transit App or follow the KC Streetcar on Instagram .
