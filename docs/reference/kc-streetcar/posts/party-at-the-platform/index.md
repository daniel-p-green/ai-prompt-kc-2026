# Party at the Platform!

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/party-at-the-platform/

## Celebrate KC’s 1st Streetcar Station Stop.

Join with Mayor Sly James, the KC Streetcar Authority and the KC Streetcar Constructors in celebration of Kansas City first Streetcar Station Stop.

When : Friday, April 24 from 5:00pm-7:00pm

What : First KC Streetcar Station Stop Celebration with food and beverages provided by Anton’s Taproom and Nara . Live music provided by the local KC Band, Frankowski.

Take a group photo or a “selfie” in the new station stop shelter, designed by HDR Engineering and built by locally-based Dimensional Innovations .

Where : First KC Streetcar Station Stop location at 1610 Main St outside of Anton’s Taproom and across from Nara.
