# Sporting KC Streetcar for the win!

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/sporting-kc/

### Sporting KC to unveil a striking new KC Streetcar Wrap

Sporting Kansas City and the KC Streetcar Authority are thrilled to announce the unveiling of the latest Sporting KC Streetcar design.

The latest Sporting KC Streetcar marks the fifth year these two organizations have teamed up to energize Kansas City’s soccer fanbase. This eye-catching collaboration will be revealed to the public on February 28 at noon, just in time for Sporting KC’s home opener against the Philadelphia Union on March 2.

WHAT : Sporting KC Streetcar Reveal (KC Streetcar #802) WHEN : Wednesday, February 28, 2024 at 12:00 p.m. WHERE : Union Station Streetcar stop at Pershing Road and Main Street

“We are delighted to once again join forces with the KC Streetcar to bring the Sporting KC spirit to the streets of Kansas City,” said Aaron Borns, Vie President with Sporting Kansas City. “This collaboration is a testament to the passion and dedication of our fans, and we can’t wait to see the city come alive with the energy of the upcoming MLS season.”

The 2024 Sporting KC Streetcar design draws inspiration from Sporting’s rich brand history, featuring a modern twist on the distinctive argyle pattern – a part of the Club’s identity since 2013. Seamlessly integrated into the exterior of the KC Streetcar, the bold and dynamic diamond motif reflects the energy of the club, its fans, and the city itself.
