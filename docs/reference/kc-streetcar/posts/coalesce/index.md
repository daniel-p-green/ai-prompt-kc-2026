# Coalesce: The KC Streetcar Runway

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/coalesce/

### When the KC Streetcar transformed into a High Fashion & Artful Runway

On Saturday, November 7, 2020, Kansas City and beyond participated in a virtual fashion show like no other. People across the country virtually joined with The Kansas City Artists Coalition for a glorious evening in celebration of the fusion of art and fashion…and all on a 77ft runway called the KC Streetcar.

Styled by WIaa Style, COALESCE is an immersive art experience where fashion and fine art come together in a breath taking collaboration celebrating fine art on the runway. Guests safely experienced how eight local Kansas City artists transformed gowns into walking canvasses of fine art with a virtual experience unlike any other.
