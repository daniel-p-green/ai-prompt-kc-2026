# Holiday Jam 2024

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/holiday-jam-2024/

### Who’s ready to jam this holiday season?

Head downtown this holiday season and enjoy free live music on board the free KC Streetcar as you explore downtown from the City Market to Union Station. The Kansas City Streetcar is hosting live musical performances on board the KC Streetcar during Saturday afternoons and Tuesday evenings from Nov 30 – Dec. 21.

Music will range from traditional holiday music, covers and original music performed by local artists. Enjoy the holidays downtown and on board the KC Streetcar this season.

How to you know which streetcar is playing music? Well, that is part of the fun. All on board performances will start at the Union Station Northbound streetcar stop. Once we take off, you can hop on the Holiday Jam Streetcar anywhere on the route during the scheduled performance time to enjoy the live music. You can track the streetcar using the Transit App or follow the KC Streetcar on Instagram .
