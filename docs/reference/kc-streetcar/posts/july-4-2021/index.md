# Fourth of July Holiday Weekend Hours of Service

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/july-4-2021/

### The KC Streetcar will operate with the following hours for the 2021 July 4th Holiday.

### – Saturday July 3rd- 7:00 a.m. – midnight.

### Four Streetcar service running from 4:00 p.m. – midnight.

### – Sunday July 4th, 7:00 a.m. – midnight.

### – Monday July 5th, 7:00 am to 10:00 pm (Holiday/Sunday schedule)

### Have a safe holiday weekend and thank you for riding the KC Streetcar.
