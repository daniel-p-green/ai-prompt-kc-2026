# Labor Day Weekend 2019

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/labor-day-weekend-2019/

The KC Streetcar will operate with the following hours during Labor Day Weekend.

- Friday, August 30: 6:00am – 2:00am
- Saturday, August 31: 7:00am – 2:00am
- Sunday, September 1: 7:00am – 11:00pm
- Monday, September 2: 7:00am – 11:00pm
More information about KC Streetcar hours can be found here https://kcstreetcar.org/route/hours-of-operation/
