# July 4th Holiday Hours

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/july-4th-holiday/

### The KC Streetcar will operate from 6:00am – Midnight for the 4th of July Holiday.

If you are heading to Riverfest at Berkley Riverfront Park, we encourage people to be safe and take the streetcar to the River Market North stop and transfer to the free shuttles heading to Riverfest. More information about Riverfest can be found on their website .
