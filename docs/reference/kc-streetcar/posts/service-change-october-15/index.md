# Service Change 10-15-16

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/service-change-october-15/

### On Saturday, October 15, 2016, the KC Streetcar will begin operations at 6:00am and operate until 2:00am, Sunday, October 16.

#### For those participating in the KC Marathon that day, please consider parking along the streetcar route taking the KC Streetcar to the race start location, which is at Crown Center . Race start time is 7:00am.
