# Halloween HAUNT

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/halloween-haunt/

## Worlds of Fun Halloween Haunt Takes Over the KC Streetcar on Friday the 13th

Worlds of Fun Halloween HAUNT Screamsters will bring mayhem to downtown Kansas City when they take over the Zambezi Zinger Streetcar on Friday, October 13 at 4:00 p.m. Zombies, demented monkeys, vampires, and maniacal creatures will board KC Streetcar 803 at 4:00 p.m. and ride until 5:00 p.m. Worlds of Fun will provide giveaways including Haunt tickets to those brave enough to ride Friday evening. Supplies are limited. WHAT : Worlds of Fun Halloween HAUNT Streetcar Takeover WHEN : Friday, October 13 4:00 – 5:00 p.m. The streetcar will depart from the Union Station Streetcar stop at 4:00 p.m. HAUNT Screamsters will make approximately two loops on board the Zambezi Zinger Streetcar WHERE : Zambezi Zinger Streetcar (803), Union Station Streetcar Stop. Track the streetcar online at www.kcstreetcar.org/route/streetcar-tracker/
