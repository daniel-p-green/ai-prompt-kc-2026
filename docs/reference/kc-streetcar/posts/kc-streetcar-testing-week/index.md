# KC Streetcar Testing This Week

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/kc-streetcar-testing-week/

## Vehicle Testing

During the week of of February 8, the KC Streetcar will be undergoing testing between the hours of 12:00 am and 6:00 am, Tuesday – Thursday. Testing will take place along Main Street between Pershing Rd and 18th Street.

During testing, Main Street will be closed from Pershing Rd to 18th Street. There will be no east or west bound traffic across Main St from 19th or 20th Streets. East and westbound traffic will be detoured to 18th Street. The testing taking place involves speed and testing of the brake systems so it is vital that Main Street is clear of other vehicles and crossing pedestrians. This is for the public’s safety as well as for the safety of the streetcar team.

For the latest updates, follow the KC Streetcar on twitter . #kcstreetcar
