# Chiefs KC Streetcar Red Friday Reveal

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/red-friday-reveal/

### Kick off Super Bowl Weekend with the Chiefs Streetcar Red Friday Rally

#### Everyone is getting into the Super Bowl spirit, including the KC Streetcar.

In partnership with the Kansas City Chiefs, the KC Streetcar Authority will reveal the Chiefs Super Bowl LVII Streetcar on Red Friday, February 10. From Noon – 3:00 p.m. the Chiefs Streetcar will be on display at the Union Station streetcar stop. Fans can come down and take photos with this one-of-a-kind streetcar wrap created specifically for the Chiefs Super Bowl LVII run. But the fun won’t stop there. At 3:00 p.m., Chiefs fans are invited to board the Chiefs Streetcar for a Red Friday Rally on wheels. The rolling parade will be led by Kansas City’s own Chiefs Rumble Drumline as the Chiefs Streetcar takes its inaugural ride downtown. The Red Friday Rally will include one loop downtown and continue inside Union Station where it will meet up with the newly expanded Chiefs Fan Zone, featuring several brilliant AlphaLit installations and other selfie-worthy Chiefs displays. While visiting the displays in Union Station, wish the team good luck by bringing cans of non-perishable food on Friday to benefit Harvesters Food Network. The Chiefs Streetcar will be in service once the Red Friday Rally begins and all weekend long, making it the best way to get to all of the Super Bowl watch parties downtown. Red Friday Rally Details:

- Noon – 3:00 p.m.: Chiefs Streetcar on display at the Union Station streetcar stop tail track
- 3:00 p.m.: Chiefs Rumble Drumline board and perform on the Chiefs Streetcar for one loop around downtown
- 3:30 p.m.: Chiefs Rumble Drumline arrives back at the Union Station streetcar stop and continues the party at the Transit Plaza
- 3:40pm – 4:00pm: Red Friday Rally with the Chiefs Rumble Drumline continues inside and through Union Station’s Grand Hall and Grand Plaza.
Want to find the Chiefs Streetcar? KC Streetcar 801 “Chiefs Streetcar” can be found using the tracker on our website: https://kcstreetcar.org/route/streetcar-tracker/
