# Chiefs Kingdom Parade 2023

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/chiefs-kingdom-parade-2023/

#### THE KC STREETCAR WILL ADJUST SERVICE ON CHIEFS KINGDOM PARADE DAY

#### Two RideKC Buses will temporarily replace streetcar vehicle service starting at 6:00 a.m.

KC Streetcar service will be adjusted on Wednesday, February 15, 2023 in order to accommodate the Chiefs Kingdom Champions Parade.

Kansas City will host the Chiefs Kingdom Champions Parade for the Super Bowl Champions, the Kansas City Chiefs, on Wednesday, February 15 at noon. The parade will be followed by a victory rally at Union Station.

In response to safety and operational considerations, and parade logistics at Union Station, the KC Streetcar will replace streetcar vehicle service with two RideKC Bus Links starting at 6:00 a.m. Wednesday, February 15. The RideKC Bus links will operate along Main Street, stopping at the following designated streetcar stops ( click below image for full map ):

- North Loop at 7th & Main
- Library at 9th & Main
- Metro Center at 12th & Main
- Power & Light at 14th & Main
- Kauffman Center at 16th & Main
- Crossroads at 19th & Main
The Union Station, City Market, River Market North, and River Market West streetcar stops will be closed to service. KC Streetcar vehicle service will resume once it is safe to do so after the parade on Wednesday.

“The KC Streetcar congratulates the Kansas City Chiefs on their World Championship. We look forward to helping make Wednesday’s parade and celebration as successful and safe as it possibly can be,”said Tom Gerend, executive director, KC Streetcar Authority.

For information about the Chiefs Kingdom Champions Parade, visit www.chiefsparade.com. You can also follow the KC Sports Commission (@SportsKC) on Twitter and Facebook.
