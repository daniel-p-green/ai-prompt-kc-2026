# Where can I park?

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/where-can-i-park/

## With on-street parking limited during construction, it’s helpful to know where the public parking lots are located.

Kansas Citians have been tremendously patient throughout streetcar construction. From private utility to water system upgrades to building track, construction has taken over the 2.2 mile stretch that will make up the downtown streetcar route.

While on-street parking may be limited in certain segments, Kansas City still has a plethora of parking lots scattered throughout the River Market, the Central Business District, and the Crossroads District. Below are three maps detailing out parking lot locations and traffic patterns.
