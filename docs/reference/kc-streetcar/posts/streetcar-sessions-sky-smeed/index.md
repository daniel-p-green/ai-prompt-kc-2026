# Streetcar Sessions with Sky Smeed

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/streetcar-sessions-sky-smeed/

On a warm February Saturday in Kansas City, Sky Smeed delighted several hundred streetcar passengers with some original music.

Sky was in town for the Annual International Folk Conference and Festival and took time out of his busy schedule to entertain us.

Thank you to 90.9 The Bridge for filming the first Streetcar Session. To learn more about Sky and his music, visit his website www.skysmeed.com
