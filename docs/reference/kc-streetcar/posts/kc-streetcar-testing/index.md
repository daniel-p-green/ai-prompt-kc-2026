# KC Streetcar Testing

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/kc-streetcar-testing/

KC Streetcar testing continues the week of November 23, 2015. The Streetcar will be cruising along Main Street and around the River Market on Monday and Tuesday . You can catch a glimpse of the streetcar on the route anytime between the hours of 9:00am – 3:00pm with a break around the lunch hour.

The public is encouraged to not park on the streetcar tracks and to please be patient with the streetcar as it is conducting testing. If you are parked alongside the streetcar route, please remember to park your entire vehicle, side mirrors included, inside the white lines.
