# Main Street Extension Meeting

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/main-street-extension-meeting/

You’re invited to a public meeting for the KC Streetcar Main Street Extension to UMKC.

View and comment on the proposed route to extend the KC Streetcar south to 51st and Main Street. Learn more about how it plans to integrate with existing bus routes and potential bicycle and pedestrian connections. Information about timeframe and financing will also be shared with the public.

The meeting will provide an overview of the project as well as an open house format where attendees can ask questions directly to project team members.

The meeting will be held on Thursday, February 21, 2019 from 4:30pm – 6:30pm at Saint Paul’s Episcopal Church at 11 East 40th Street, KCMO 64111. Public transit is available.
