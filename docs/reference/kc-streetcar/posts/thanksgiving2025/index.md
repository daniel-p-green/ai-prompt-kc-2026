# Thanksgiving Hours

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/thanksgiving2025/

### The KC Streetcar will operate with the following hours during the Thanksgiving Holiday:

Thanksgiving Day, Thursday, November 27: 7:00 a.m. – Midnight

The KC Streetcar will provide boosted service during peak times on Thanksgiving Day to accommodate the Plaza Lighting Ceremony.

Friday, November 29: 5:00 a.m. – 1:00 a.m. Saturday, November 30: 5:00 a.m. – 1:00 a.m. Sunday, December 1: 5:00 a.m. – Midnight
