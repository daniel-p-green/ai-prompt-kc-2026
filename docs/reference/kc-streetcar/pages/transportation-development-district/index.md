# Downtown TDD

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/transportation-development-district/

### Downtown Transportation Development District (TDD)

The Downtown KC Streetcar (starter line) is funded by the Downtown Transportation Development District through a combination of sales tax, property assessments, and parking lot assessments. The Downtown TDD is a separate entity from the Kansas City Streetcar Authority and the City of Kansas City, Missouri, and is governed by a Board of Directors.
