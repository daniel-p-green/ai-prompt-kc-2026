# KC Streetcar Stories

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/route/kcstreetcarstories/

The KC Streetcar is more than just a transportation vehicle, getting people from point A to point B. The KC Streetcar is also about making social and economic connections; it’s about the people that use the streetcar as a part of their daily lives. We call this KC Streetcar Stories .
