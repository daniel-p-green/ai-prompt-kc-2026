# Hours of Operation

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/route/hours-of-operation/

### Sunday – Thursday: 5:00 a.m. – Midnight

### Friday: 5:00 a.m. – 1:00 a.m.

### Saturday: 5:00 a.m. – 1:00 a.m.

The KC Streetcar is the most frequent transit service in the region, with streetcars running every 10-15 minutes during peak periods, seven days a week. During off peak hours, streetcars run every 12 to 18 minutes. The KC Streetcar is free to ride.

The administrative and operations staff of the KC Streetcar Authority are located at the Kite Singleton Vehicle Maintenance Facility at 600 East 3rd Street. The office is open Monday through Friday, 8am to 5pm.

The KC Streetcar does operate on holidays but on an adjusted schedule. The designated holidays for KC Streetcar service are:

- New Year’s Day
- Memorial Day
- Fourth of July
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving Day
- Christmas Day
