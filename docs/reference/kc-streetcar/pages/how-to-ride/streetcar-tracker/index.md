# Streetcar Tracker

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/how-to-ride/streetcar-tracker/

Streetcars operate 7 days a week, with 10-15 minute arrival frequencies. However, with heavy traffic and increased ridership, the wait times may be slightly longer. Need to know when the streetcar is arriving? In addition to the digital real time arrival signs at the streetcar stops, which let passengers know when the next streetcar will arrive, you can now track the streetcar’s location when behind your computer or on your phone.

- For tracking from your cell phones, Transit gives you upcoming departure times for all nearby transit lines – RideKC buses, the KC Streetcar…even Uber. This app also allows you to plan your trip across multiple transit options. Download for your phone here: Transit
- Streetcar tracking and arrival information is NOW available on Apple Maps and Google Maps.
