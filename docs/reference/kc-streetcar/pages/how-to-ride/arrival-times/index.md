# Streetcar Arrivals

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/how-to-ride/arrival-times/

Want to know when the KC Streetcar will arrive at your nearest stop? Want to know if you have enough time to grab a coffee before boarding? Well now you can get live arrival streetcar feeds on your desktop, laptop, tablet, TV and phone for free. Businesses – this is a great feature for your customers. Property owners – your residents and tenants would love this. You need only three things to host a KC Streetcar Arrivals display:

- Computer with an internet connection
- Google Chrome web browser (Chrome works best but other browsers will support this feature)
- 16:9 TV or monitor (720p/1080p/4K)
Get your customized link below and paste it into the browser. Set it as a bookmark or home page so you can restart it easily later. Put Chrome into full-screen mode and turn off the “Always Show Toolbar in Fullscreen” setting.

- River Market North Stop: https://arrivals.kcstreetcar.org/rmn
- River Market West Stop: https://arrivals.kcstreetcar.org/rmw
- City Market Stop: https://arrivals.kcstreetcar.org/cmk
- North Loop Stops: https://arrivals.kcstreetcar.org/nlnnls
- Library Stops: https://arrivals.kcstreetcar.org/lbnlbs
- Metro Center Stops: https://arrivals.kcstreetcar.org/mcnmcs
- Power & Light Stops: https://arrivals.kcstreetcar.org/plnpls
- Kauffman Center Stops: https://arrivals.kcstreetcar.org/kcnkcs
- Crossroads Stops: https://arrivals.kcstreetcar.org/crncrs
- Union Station Stops: https://arrivals.kcstreetcar.org/usnuss
- WWI Museum & Memorial Stops: https://arrivals.kcstreetcar.org/wwnwws
- Union Hill Stops: https://arrivals.kcstreetcar.org/uhnuhs
- Armour Stops: https://arrivals.kcstreetcar.org/arnars
- Westport Stops: https://arrivals.kcstreetcar.org/wpnwps
- Southmoreland Stops: https://arrivals.kcstreetcar.org/smnsms
- Art Museums Stops: https://arrivals.kcstreetcar.org/amnams
- Plaza Stops: https://arrivals.kcstreetcar.org/pznpzs
- UMKC Stops: https://arrivals.kcstreetcar.org/umn
That’s it! Turn off your screensaver and power-saving settings to keep the display active. There’s no maintenance required and upgrades are automatic; arrival times automatically update throughout the day and we’ll refresh your screen remotely when new features are available.
