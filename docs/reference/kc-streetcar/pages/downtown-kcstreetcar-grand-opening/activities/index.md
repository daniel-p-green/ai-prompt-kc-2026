# Free to party

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/downtown-kcstreetcar-grand-opening/activities/

KC Streetcar opening weekend is about celebrating the next chapter in Kansas City’s storied history. On Friday, May 6 and Saturday, May 7, enjoy fireworks both nights, carnival rides, neighborhood street parties, entertainment, dining, shopping, arts and more! It’s all part of a re-energized Downtown Kansas City and the connectivity that the KC Streetcar provides! More information will be added as it’s available.

Connect, discover and explore! It’s #timetoridekc!

## Friday May 6

### Union Station

10:00 a.m. – KC Streetcar Opening Celebration Join Kansas City’s Mayor Sly James, dignitaries and city officials at the Grand Opening ceremony as the KC Streetcar officially begins service!

11:00 a.m. – KC Streetcar service begins Free to ride, with 16 platform stops connecting Downtown Kansas City’s neighborhoods, residents, visitors, businesses and more, KC Streetcar service will run until 2 a.m. on Friday’s and Saturday’s!

11:00 a.m. – Carnival Rides at Union Station presented by Sprint What’s a party without a ferris wheel? Check out the sites from high above or take a ride around the Merry Go Round! Best of all? Carnival rides are like the KC Streetcar … Free to Ride, courtesy of Sprint! (Carnival will run from 11:00 a.m. – 10:00 p.m.)

11:00 a.m. – The Project H The Project H will take your ears and your feet for a ride! This 7 piece instrumental group will get you moving whether you are sitting down or already moving. They’ve played with reggae bands, rappers, folk acts, hard rock bands and everything in between.

5:00 p.m. – Run With It Built on a foundation of funk and soul, Run With It caters to those who enjoy a little bit of everything. Whether its One Republic, Maroon 5, Kings of Leon, Stevie Wonder or Bruno Mars, this energetic trio will leave their unique pop-rock mark!

7:30 p.m. – The Zeros presented by Herzog Transit The Zeros are Kansas City’s best party band! The Zeros will lead the way with non-stop fun for all ages, followed by fireworks from the National World War I Museum and Memorial.

9:00 p.m. – Fireworks presented by KC Streetcar Constructors Watch from Union Station as we celebrate the debut of the KC Streetcar with a special nighttime fireworks show over the lawn of the National WWI Museum and Memorial!

9:00 p.m. – A Streetcar Named Desire presented by Friends of the River Pack a snack, grab a chair and blanket and watch the 1951 film classic under the stars on the big screen. Presented by Friends of the River, the movie will be shown in the park just northwest of the City Market, adjacent to 3rd Street.
