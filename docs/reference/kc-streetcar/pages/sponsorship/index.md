# Sponsorship/Advertising

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/sponsorship/

## Interested in sponsorship/advertising opportunities with the KC Streetcar?

### Contact info@kcstreetcar.org and someone will be in touch with you.

Streetcars do more than get people from point A to point B. They drive development, attract new businesses, employees, and residents, and help shape the future of public transit in Kansas City. Through a variety of programs and partnership opportunities, businesses, community organizations, and brands can tap into the power of the KC Streetcar to connect and grow.

- You can sponsor/advertise at a KC Streetcar stop
- You can wrap a streetcar vehicle if you are an eligible institution or event
Sponsorship/Advertising opportunities are available at the streetcar stops along the route between UMKC and the River Market. In total, there are 30 streetcar stops on the route from 3rd Street to 51st Street, with the Plaza Transit Hub offering four advertising options (four shelters) at that location. The only stop without a shelter is Metro Center Southbound at 12th & Main Street.
