# Singleton Yard Expansion

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/about-streetcar/mainstreet-extension/singleton-yard-expansion/

The KC Streetcar Extension includes the expansion of the existing Singleton Yard Maintenance Facility. Renovations began in late 2022 to better accommodate the larger streetcar fleet and related operational and maintenance needs. Construction was completed in early 2024.
