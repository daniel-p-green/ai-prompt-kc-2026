## Opening October 24, 2025

Construction is nearly complete and streetcars are operating along the extension…but without passengers. What’s going on?

Before we can open up the KC Streetcar Main Street Extension for passenger service, the team must conduct an extensive series of federally mandated tests of the entire system. Some of these tests include, but are not limited to:

- Traffic and trains signals
- Power Substations and electrical systems
- Streetcar and platform interface
- Streetcar and bus interface, especially at the Plaza Transit stop
- Schedule verification and validation
- Passenger Information System verification
- High-speeds and heavy loads
- Multi-Modal and mobility scenarios
- Emergency scenarios like evacuations, route incidents, loss of streetcar power, etc
- Operator training and familiarization
The testing phase ensures that the KC Streetcar is safe for all passengers prior to opening October 24, 2025. Let’s Ride!

### March 2025 Update:

Starting in late March 2025, the KC Streetcar Constructors started the massive repaving of Main Street between Linwood Blvd and 49th Street. This will smooth out the entire roadway outside of the trackways, followed by permanent re-striping of the entire alignment.

On Friday, January 8, 2021, the Federal Transit Administration (FTA) and the City of Kansas City, Mo., officially signed the Full Federal Funding Agreement (FFGA) for the KC Streetcar Main Street Extension at a brief ceremony at the KC Streetcar Singleton Yard.
