# Board & Committee Meetings

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/meetings/

The Kansas City Streetcar Authority (KCSA) is a not-for-profit organization formed for the purpose of managing, operating, and maintaining the streetcar. In addition to overseeing streetcar operations, the KCSA supports system branding, marketing, public communication, and community engagement.

### KCSA Board Meetings

The KCSA Executive Board generally meets every other month on a Thursday from 12pm-2:00pm . These meetings are held at the Singleton Yard at 600 East 3rd Street, Kansas City, Missouri, and open to the public. Click the below dates for meeting agendas and meeting minutes.
