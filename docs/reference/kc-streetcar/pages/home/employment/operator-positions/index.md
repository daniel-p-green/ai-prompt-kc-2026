# Operator Positions

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/home/employment/operator-positions/

KC Streetcar is looking for Operators! Join our team and do something different and exciting as you play an important role — the face of KC Streetcar. Here you can earn while you learn to master a job that is fun, stable and predictable. Your next stop? A chance to love what you do … at KC Streetcar!

To become one of our KC Streetcar Operators, you’ll need to be a master of focus, observation and efficiency — and also great with people. Not only will you operate the streetcar: You’ll serve as our eyes and ears in the field. That includes watching for and reporting road vehicles or work that could impede movement along the line, any defects or concerns with the tracks, stops or cars and much more.

Please contact Herzog Transit Services Recruiter, Chloe Ungs at 816-385-8263 or cungs@herzog.com , for more information about open positions.
