# Safety and Security Manager Position

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/home/employment/safety-and-security-manager-position/

KC Streetcar is looking to grow our team with a Safety and Security Manager! Join the KC Streetcar team to do something unique and exciting as you play an important role in KC’s future. Enjoy a job where every day is different, you can earn while you learn, and you have opportunities to advance and grow right along with us. Your next stop? A chance to love what you do … at KC Streetcar!

As a Safety and Security Manager, you’ll put your experience to work as you help ensure safe operation of the streetcars, facilities and our team. Your responsibilities will include establishing safety procedures and programs, conducting safety inspections, participating in emergency response drills and more. Also, you’ll also operate the KC Streetcars from time to time. If you have a passion for safety, love managing the details and are a people person, read the full Safety and Security Manager job listing and apply today .

Please contact Herzog Transit Services Recruiter, Madisyn Riley at MRiley@herzog.com , for more information about open positions.
