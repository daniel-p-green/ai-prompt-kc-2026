# Maintenance Positions

Source: https://kcstreetcar.org/home/employment/maintenance-positions/

KC Streetcar is looking to grow our maintenance team! Join the KC Streetcar team to do something unique and exciting as you play an important role in KC’s future. Enjoy a job where every day is different, you can earn while you learn, and you have opportunities to advance and grow right along with us. Your next stop? A chance to love what you do … at KC Streetcar!

To join our dedicated team as a Service Maintainer you’ll need a knowledge of tools and equipment and mechanical or electrical experience to keep the streetcars and facilities maintained and running 24/7/365. You’ll also keep the tracks and cars clean, ensure safety practices are met, and regularly interface with KC Streetcar passengers and representatives. If you’re the type of person who can perform quality work efficiently with minimal supervision, read the full Service Maintainer job listing and apply today.

#### The salary range for a Service Maintainer position is: $19.00/hr – $21.60/hour plus benefits including health care & a 401K match.

Please contact Herzog Transit Services Recruiter, Chloe Ungs at 816-344-4584 or cungs@herzog.com , for more information about open positions.
